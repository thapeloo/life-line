01 About

Who We Are

About Lifeline
Pharmacy

Lifeline Pharmacy is a trusted retail pharmacy based in Phakalane, Gaborone, Botswana. We provide affordable, high-quality medications and healthcare products to our local community.

Our team of qualified pharmacists ensures that every customer receives accurate prescriptions, professional service, and reliable health advice — because your health is our priority.

06 Brand Voice

How We Communicate

Our voice is built on four foundational pillars that guide every word we publish.

Trustworthy

We speak with confidence and accuracy. Our customers rely on us for correct information — every word we publish reflects that responsibility.

Caring

We write warmly and with empathy. Health is personal — our tone should reflect that we genuinely care about the wellbeing of every person we serve.

Clear

We avoid jargon. Complex healthcare information is translated into plain, accessible language so every customer — regardless of background — feels informed.

Community-First

We are proudly rooted in Phakalane. Our voice reflects local pride and genuine investment in the health of the community we serve every day.

Photography

Imagery Style

Our imagery captures authentic healthcare moments — professional, warm, and community-focused. Images should always be high quality, contextually relevant, and reflect the diversity of the Phakalane community.

Use imagery that shows

  • Real healthcare interactions
  • Diverse, local community members
  • Clean, professional environments
  • Warm, approachable expressions

Avoid imagery that is

  • Staged or overly stock-photo generic
  • Dark, clinical, or cold in tone
  • Inconsistent with brand colour story
  • Low resolution or poorly cropped
Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Use Official Colour Palette

    Ensure you use the brand's specified colours in all assets, digital and print.

  • Typography Consistency

    Use Montserrat with the specified weights and sizes in the brand guidelines for all communications.

  • Follow Imagery Style

    Use photography that is authentic, high quality, and reflects the diversity of our community.

  • Consistent Messaging

    Ensure the message delivered is aligned with our brand values of trust, care, clarity, and community.

Don'ts

  • Modify the Logo

    Do not alter, distort, rotate, recolour, or change the logo in any way without permission.

  • Misuse Design Elements

    Avoid using brand graphic elements in a way that is not consistent with the guidelines.

  • Inappropriate Colour Usage

    Do not use colours outside the official palette for any brand representation.

  • Conflicting Messages

    Avoid communications that contradict or obscure our brand values and voice.

Legal Guidelines

Legal Guidelines

Protecting the Lifeline Pharmacy brand is a shared responsibility. These guidelines ensure our brand remains legally secure and properly represented.

01

Copyright

All materials produced related to our brand are protected by copyright. Always ensure to obtain permission before using third-party owned materials.

02

Trademark

Logos, slogans, and other brand elements have been registered as trademarks. The use of the ™ or ® symbols must always follow established rules.

03

Licensing

Some assets may be used under specific licenses. Ensure to follow all terms of these licenses, including usage, attribution, and restrictions.

04

Unauthorised Use

Avoid unauthorised or potentially brand-damaging use of our brand assets. All usage must be pre-approved.

05

Objections & Violations

Immediately report any objections or violations against our brand to the appropriate department for swift action.

06

Marketing Approval

All marketing materials must be approved before distribution to ensure compliance with applicable guidelines and legal regulations.
